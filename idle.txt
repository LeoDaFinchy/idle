Let's make a game!
  name:My Totally Original Game
  
Resources
  *charm
    name: Charm
    
  *cash
    name: Cash
  
  *brine
    name: Brine
  
Buttons
  *charmButton
    name: Charm!
    on click:gain 1 charm
    
  *sellBrineButton
    name: Sell Brine
    req:(brine >= 1)
    on click:gain (brine * 0.1) cash
    on click:lose (brine) brine

Buildings
  *visitor
    cost:10 charm
    passive:gain 0.1 brine
    
  *member
    cost:100 charm,1 visitor
    on tick:gain 1 brine

  *regular
    cost:1000 charm,1 member
    on tick:gain 10 brine
    
  *staff
    cost:100 cash
    on tick:if(cash >= 1)gain 1 charm
      else
lose 1 staff
      end
    
Upgrades
  *towels
    cost:100 cash
    passive:multiply yield of brine by 2
  *heaters
    cost:1000 cash
    passive:multiply yield of brine by 2
